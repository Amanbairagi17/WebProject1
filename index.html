The Indian Cricket Team, known as the Men in Blue, represents India in international cricket. Governed by the BCCI, it has a rich legacy with legendary players like Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli. Known for its passionate fanbase, the team has achieved numerous milestones, including World Cup victories in 1983 and 2011.
